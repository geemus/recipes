## Moist Banana Bread

1 loaf = 10 minutes active + 1 hour bake

* 3 very ripe bananas (medium/large)
* 1/2 cup unsalted butter (8 tbsp) at room temp
* 3/4 cup granulated sugar
* 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
* 1-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
* 1 tsp baking soda
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
* 1 cup walnuts
* 1/2 cup raisins

### Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
2. Grease and flour a loaf pan (9.25" x 5.25" x 2.75")
3. (optionally) lightly roast walnuts on a skillet, continuously stirring to avoid burning.
4. Cream together 8 Tbsp softened butter and 3/4 cup sugar (or honey if using).
5. Mash bananas with a fork until the consistency of chunky applesauce and add them to the batter along with 2 eggs, mixing until blended.
6. In a separate bowl, whisk together: 1-1/2 cups of flour, 1 tsp of baking soda and 1/2 tsp of salt then add to batter.
7. Add 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract and mix in chopped walnuts and raisins.
8. Pour into prepared loaf pan.
9. Bake at 350F for 55-60 or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
10. Let rest for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool.
