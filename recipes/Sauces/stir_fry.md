## Stir-Fry Sauce

? - 5 minutes

* 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
* 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine
* 1 tbsp distilled white vinegar
* 2 tbsp chicken stock
* 1 tbsp sugar
* 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
* 2 tsp cornstarch

1. Whisk together, add to finished stir fry, stir to coat and thicken.
