## Sous Vide Lentils

* water (100% = 2 parts)
* lentil (50% = 1 part)
* salt (1% = 1g per 100g water)

1. Preheat bath to desired temp (185F/85C firmer to 194F/90C softer).j:w
2. Cook for 1 hour.
3. Cool lentils in cooking liquid.
4. Strain off excess liquid for storage and usage.
